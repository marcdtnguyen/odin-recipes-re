Stir Fry

Description

For this beef and green bean stir-fry, you’ll want to have all your ingredients ready to go, because once you start cooking, you’ll need less than 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Sirloin
  • Baking Soda
  • Cornstarch
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Shaoxing wine
  • Chinese 5-spice powder
  • White pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Beef stock
  • Sesame oil
  • Rice
  • Sesame seeds
